About Me

I am Mbungai Marie Josette Leinyuy, known by my stage name Leinyuy The Poet. At 20 years old, I'm a third-year Computer Engineering student at the National Higher Polytechnic with a passion for both technology and creative arts.

I combine technical skills in UI/UX design, front-end development, and full-stack development with creativity as an award-winning spoken word artist, poet, and debater. I thrive on creating meaningful projects that merge technology, design, and storytelling while leading initiatives that make a positive impact in my community.
